NeoHookeanMaterial

ONSAS.NeoHookeanMaterial — Module

Module defining a Neo-Hookean hyper-elastic material.

source
ONSAS.NeoHookeanMaterial.NeoHookean — Type

Material with Neo-Hookean properties. The strain energy Ψ is: Ψ(𝔼) = G/2 (tr(ℂ) -2 *log(J))^2 + K/2 (J - 1)^2

For context see the wikipedia article on Neo-Hookean_solid.

It is also possible to construct a NeoHookean material given its elasticity and shear modulus E, ν respectively and its density ρ. For context see the wikipedia article on Lamé parameters.
